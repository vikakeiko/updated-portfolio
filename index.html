About Me

Hello! I am a student at UCLA's coding boot camp. I grew up in Japan for most of my life and Japanese is my native language. In my university in Tokyo, I studied English and Aviation Management. Then I worked in the most exclusive company in the Middle East as a flight attendant. My time spent living in Japan, Australia, Dubai, and the USA, I have had the privilege of seeing the world through many perspectives: political economy, medical care, and the everyday lifestyle. Through my experience, I've learned to adjust to a new environment quickly with many people who have different backgrounds and culture. In addition to my interest in traveling and learning new languages, I enjoy art as a hobby and I display my artistic value through additional hobbies in food, architecture, fashion and beauty.
